The short B-ball story

There once was this kid and his name was...
Basketball. Everyone clowned on him because his name was basketball. Even his own family
laughed at his name, despite this constant torment from even his loved ones he decided that we
would live past it. He tried out for other sports like volleyball and tennis, unfortuneately he
learned he wasn't very athletic so his attempts were an utter failure. Eventually the overwhelming
weight of knowing that he wasn't good at any other sport pushed him to face his cruciable. He decided to
play basketball. From there his life worstened because when he attempted to play he soon relized that
basketball might have been his name, but it was not his game. He failed horribly at that as well. Years
past by and ironically Basketball became a basketball coach, for all his hate of his name he found a
passion to growing and understanding the game. He eventually met a women her name was Tabletennis, and
Basketball called her Ping-Pong for short. They got married settled down and had child. Remembering all
the harsh treatment they were surcommed to they decided to name their child after something other than a
sport, they named their child Comedy.
